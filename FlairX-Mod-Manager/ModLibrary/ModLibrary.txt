Put your mod folders in the appropriate game and category subdirectories:

NEW STRUCTURE (Category-based):
- ZZ/[Category]/[ModName]/ for Zenless Zone Zero mods
- WW/[Category]/[ModName]/ for Wuthering Waves mods  
- SR/[Category]/[ModName]/ for Star Rail mods
- GI/[Category]/[ModName]/ for Genshin Impact mods
- HI/[Category]/[ModName]/ for Honkai Impact mods

Examples:
- GI/Characters/Ayaka_Outfit/
- GI/UI/Better_Menu/
- GI/Weapons/Cool_Sword/
- ZZ/Characters/Belle_Mod/
- SR/Characters/March_Outfit/

Categories can be anything you want (Characters, UI, Weapons, etc.)
Categories will appear as menu items in FlairX for easy navigation.
